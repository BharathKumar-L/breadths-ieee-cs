RAJALAKSHMI ENGINEERING COLLEGE...

Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC) is a premier institution committed to excellence in engineering education, research, and innovation. Our focus on advancing technology is exemplified by our active involvement in cutting-edge projects and our dedication to fostering a culture of innovation. This commitment is highlighted by our successful organization of an IEEE International Conference in the past, which brought together scholars, industry experts, and students from around the world. Building on this momentum, we are planning multiple events to engage our students actively. Our vibrant IEEE Student Chapter, with societies spanning various departments, plays a pivotal role in fostering innovation and collaboration across disciplines.


IEEE STUDENT BRANCH...

The IEEE Student Branches at Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC) play a crucial role in encouraging innovation, collaboration, and technological advancement among students. These branches, which include societies such as IEEE Antennas and Propagation Society (AP-S), IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (CIS), IEEE Communications Society, IEEE Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (EMBS), IEEE Computer Society (CS), IEEE Microwave Theory and Techniques Society (MTTS), IEEE Power and Energy Society (PES), IEEE Power Electronics Society (PELS), IEEE Photonics Society, IEEE Reliability Society (RA-S), IEEE Technology and Engineering Management Society (TEMS), and IEEE Vehicular Technology Society (VTS), operate across various departments. They offer a platform for students to participate in technical activities, workshops, seminars, and competitions that enhance their knowledge and skills. The branches organize events that bring together students, faculty, and industry professionals, providing opportunities for networking, mentorship, and professional growth. Through these initiatives, REC's IEEE Student Branches contribute significantly to the academic and professional development of students, preparing them to succeed in the ever-evolving field of engineering and technology.


IEEE BREADTHS...

IEEE Breadths is a revolutionary symposium organized by all 12 IEEE societies at Rajalakshmi Engineering College. This event invites both IEEE members and non-IEEE members from across the globe to participate, providing a unique networking opportunity and offering a plethora of events that cater to diverse interests and expertise in the field of engineering and technology. The symposium is supported by the following societies: IEEE Antennas and Propagation Society (AP-S), IEEE Computational Intelligence Society (CIS), IEEE Communications Society, IEEE Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (EMBS), IEEE Computer Society (CS), IEEE Microwave Theory and Techniques Society (MTTS), IEEE Power and Energy Society (PES), IEEE Power Electronics Society (PELS), IEEE Photonics Society, IEEE Reliability Society (RA-S), IEEE Technology and Engineering Management Society (TEMS), and IEEE Vehicular Technology Society (VTS). Each society contributes to the diverse range of activities and sessions, ensuring a comprehensive exploration of advanced technologies and facilitating meaningful connections among participants.
